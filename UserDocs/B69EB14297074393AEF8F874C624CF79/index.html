Registering as a Current EDS Customer

☰

Welcome to the EDS Customer Portal

This guide is designed to help you navigate registering on the new Customer Portal as an existing Extra Duty Solutions Customer.

Getting Started

  1. Visit the EDS Customer Portal login page.


  2. Click "Forgot Password."
  3. The customer Portal will direct you to the Recover Password Screen, and prompt you to enter your email address. Enter your email address and press "Email me a recovery code".



  4. You will receive an email with a recovery code. Enter the recovery code, and press "Verify Code". It may take a couple of minutes to receive the email with your recovery code.
  5. Once the recovery code has been entered, you will be directed to a screen to change your password. The password must be 8 or more characters, and contain at least 1 upper and lower case letter, 1 number, and 1 special character.
  6. Enter your new password twice, and press "Change Password>.
  7. Once your password has successfully changed, you'll return to the login screen. Enter your new credentials and press "Login".



  8. Upon login, you will be directed to the basic information page, where you'll verify your basic information, including Business Name, First and Last Name, email address, and phone number. You'll also be asked to choose your Customer Type from a list of options which include:

    • Individual
    • Private Company or LLC
    • Publicly Traded Company
    • Public Affiliated Entity

  9. After Basic Information and correct Customer type are verified, press the "Billing" button to proceed to the Billing Information tab.



  10. In the Billing section, you will be able to confirm: Billing Contact First and Last Name, Billing Address, Billing email, and Billing Phone Number. Once you have validated that this information is correct, press the "Submit" button. If this information is not correct, contact our Finance department at (203) 202-3991 ext 1.

  11. After validating your basic information, You'll be prompted to choose payment terms. You will be able to choose from a list of options that correspond to the type of entity your company is.



  12. Once you've selected from your list of available payment terms, select "Use Bank Account Form" or "Use Credit Card Form" to enter a payment method. Publicly traded or Public Entities will be press skip to proceed to the next step.



  13. Once your bank account or credit card info has been accurately entered, select submit. You will proceed to a confirmation screen informing you that you've successfully registered, and will be able to select "Go to Dashboard".

    14. If you have questions or need additional assistance, reach out to your EDS Account Management team, or call (203) 202-3991 ext 1.
