Welcome to the EDS Customer Portal

This guide is designed to help you navigate and make the most of the Extra Duty Solutions (EDS) Customer Portal. Whether you're looking to request off-duty details, manage payments, or view invoices, this guide will provide step-by-step instructions and helpful tips.

Home

The left-side navigation toolbar contains all major actions you can take in the EDS Customer Portal. Each item leads to a corresponding page where you can perform specific tasks related to managing your account and services. Below is a walkthrough of the different sections:

  • Home: The home page provides a snapshot of your account status. From here, you can quickly access important tasks like managing details and payments.
  • Scheduled Details: This section allows you to review, cancel, and edit upcoming scheduled details. You'll be able to view the details you have requested or scheduled.
  • List of Payment Methods: Manage your payment methods for paying invoices. You can add a new payment method or choose from the existing ones.
  • Invoices: View and pay invoices for services. This section provides a list of all invoices associated with your account, allowing you to review and settle any outstanding payments.
  • Payments: Review your payment history in this section. You can see all transactions, including past payments made through the portal.
  • Request a Detail: Submit a request for a new off-duty detail. Fill in the required information to schedule and request the service.
  • Detail History: View your history of requested and completed details. This section helps you track past services for your records.
  • Profile Menu: This area allows you to manage your account details. Update your personal and business information, including your contact details and password.

Getting Started

This section helps new customers get set up quickly by explaining how to create an account, and for existing customers, how to log in and re-register.

For New Customers: Follow the steps below to create a new account:

  1. Visit the EDS Customer Portal login page.
  2. Click "Register as New Customer."
  3. Complete the registration form and submit your details.
  4. EDS will review your registration and grant you access within 30 minutes (during business hours) or 8 hours (off-hours).

For New EDS Customers: Click on Register as a New Customer.

  • Fill Out the Registration Form:

    On the Customer Registration page, you will be prompted to enter your information:

    • First Name
    • Last Name
    • Email Address: Ensure you provide a valid email as this will be used for future communication and password recovery.
    • Phone Number: Provide a phone number for contact purposes.
    • Zip Code: Enter the zip code associated with your organization or service area.
  • Review and Submit:

    After completing all the fields, make sure to review the entered information for accuracy. Fields marked with a red asterisk (*) are required to proceed.

    Once the form is complete, click Submit.

  • Await Account Approval:

    Once you’ve submitted your registration, a notification will confirm that your registration request has been received. The EDS Account Management Team will review your registration request. Approvals are typically processed within 30 minutes during business hours or within 8 hours outside business hours (e.g., nights or weekends).

  • Account Approval and Access:

    After your registration is approved, you will receive a confirmation email with instructions on how to log in. Once logged in, you will have access to the portal's features, including managing scheduled details, payments, invoices, and requests for services.

    Scheduled Details Section Overview:
    The Scheduled Details section allows you to review, manage, and modify upcoming scheduled details. In this section, you'll find detailed information about each scheduled detail, including dates, locations, assigned officers, and more. You also have the ability to search, filter, and customize the table view to fit your preferences.

    Main Features of the Scheduled Details Section:
    This section allows you to view, cancel, and edit upcoming scheduled details. You can view information such as:

    • Start and end date/time
    • Location of the detail
    • Number of available openings and filled positions
    • Officers assigned to the detail
    • Agency and customer information
    • Contact details and notes

    Functions Above the Toolbar:

    • Refresh: Updates the displayed list of scheduled details to reflect the most recent changes or updates made by other users or the system.
    • Detail History: Provides access to a historical view of completed or canceled scheduled details, allowing you to review past details for reference or reporting.
    • Cancel: Allows you to cancel an ongoing action or process, or a specific scheduled detail that is selected.

    Options Below the Toolbar:

    • Search: A quick search option to find specific scheduled details based on keywords such as agency name, customer name, or officer information.
    • Filters: Filter the list of scheduled details based on various criteria like date range, location, or officer assignments.
    • Groups: Group the scheduled details by specific fields like agency name, location, or assigned officers.
    • Table Settings: Customize the table by adding, removing, or rearranging columns.
    • Save Settings: Save your customized table settings, filters, and groupings.

    Details Table Breakdown:

    • Start Date / End Date: Displays the start and end dates for the scheduled details.
    • Start Time / End Time: Shows the start and end times for the detail.
    • Location: The physical location where the scheduled detail is taking place.
    • Openings / Filled: Number of open positions and filled positions for officers.
    • Officers: Displays the list of officers assigned to the scheduled detail.
    • Agency Name: The law enforcement agency responsible for the scheduled detail.
    • Customer Name: The customer associated with the scheduled detail.
    • Holds: Indicates if there are any holds or restrictions on the detail.
    • Contact Information (Name, Email, Phone): Displays the contact information for the detail.
    • Notes: Any additional information or instructions related to the scheduled detail.

    Request a Detail Button:
    In the bottom-left corner of the Scheduled Details page, you’ll find the Request a Detail button. Clicking this button redirects you to the Request a Detail function, where you can initiate and submit a new off-duty detail request.

    How to Use the Scheduled Details Section:

    • Viewing Details: Browse through the scheduled details and view important information such as start/end dates, locations, and officer assignments.
    • Searching for Details: Use the Search option to quickly locate specific details by entering keywords like customer name or officer information.
    • Filtering and Grouping Details: Use the Filters option to narrow down your list based on date ranges, location, or other criteria.
    • Customizing the Table View: Adjust the Table Settings to show or hide columns, and use Save Settings to retain your customized view.
    • Managing Scheduled Details: By selecting the 3 dots in the upper right hand corner of the table, you can choose to edit or cancel a detail. Editing the detail will open the detail and allow you to update each of the tabs of information used to request the detail.

    List of Payment Methods Guide

    Overview:
    The List of Payment Methods section allows you to view and manage the payment methods linked to your account. You can add new methods, edit existing ones, or remove methods that are no longer needed.

    Main Features of the List of Payment Methods Section:
    In this section, you can:

    • View your saved payment methods
    • Choose which payment method to use for invoices and payments
    • Add a new ACH or credit card payment method
    • Edit or remove existing payment methods

    Functions Above the Toolbar:

    • Search: Allows you to quickly find specific payment methods by entering keywords like the payment method name or type.
    • Filters: Filter the list of payment methods based on the method type, last four digits, or other details.
    • Groups: Group the payment methods by type, last four digits, or other attributes.
    • Table Settings: Customize the table by adding, removing, or rearranging columns to better view your payment methods.

    Adding a Payment Method:
    Use the buttons at the bottom of the page to add a new ACH or credit card. When adding a payment method, you'll be prompted to enter the necessary details, including account or card information, and the payment method type.

    Managing Payment Methods:
    You can edit or delete payment methods from this section. Select a payment method and choose the appropriate action, such as editing the details or removing the method entirely.

    Invoices Guide

    Overview:
    The Invoices section allows you to view and manage your invoices. You can track payment statuses, view invoice details, and make payments directly through the portal.

    Main Features of the Invoices Section:
    In this section, you can:

    • View the status of your invoices (paid, unpaid, or overdue)
    • Check important details such as invoice numbers, dates, due dates, and balance amounts
    • Make payments for unpaid invoices
    • Filter and group invoices based on their status or payment history

    Functions Above the Toolbar:

    • Search: Find invoices using keywords such as invoice number, customer name, or agency name.
    • Filters: Narrow down your list of invoices by applying filters like status, due date, or balance due.
    • Groups: Group invoices by customer, status, or other details for easier viewing.
    • Table Settings: Customize the columns in your invoice list to display the information you need.

    Managing Invoices:
    You can view more details about each invoice by selecting it from the list. Additionally, use the "View Payment" button to see payment history for specific invoices, and the "Refresh List" button to update the list with the latest invoice data.

    Payment Options:
    You can make payments directly from this section by selecting an unpaid invoice and clicking "View Payment." Follow the instructions to complete your payment using one of your saved payment methods or by adding a new payment method.

    Request a Detail

    The Request a Detail section allows registered business entities to request off-duty details to be filled by local law enforcement agencies. This process ensures that all necessary information is provided, from the contact details to the specific requirements of the detail, such as location, number of officers, and scheduling. Each step guides users through a straightforward process, making it easy to request services while ensuring the agency has all the information they need to approve and assign officers to the detail. Follow the steps below to complete your detail request.

    Step 1: Select the Request Detail Button

    On the Scheduled Details page, locate the Request a Detail button at the bottom-left corner and click it to start the detail request process.

    Step 2: Pick a Business Entity


    A list of business entities or agencies your company is registered with will appear in the dropdown. Select the appropriate business entity for your request. Once selected, click Review > Submit to confirm your selection.

    Step 3: Enter Contact Information


    Fill in the required Contact Name, Phone Number, and Email Address for the person responsible for this detail. This contact will be used for any questions or follow-ups regarding the detail. After entering the information, click Additional Information to proceed.

    Step 4: Additional Information (Custom Agency Questions)


    Some agencies may require specific information to be entered during the detail request. These fields may include:

    • Expected number of people at the event.
    • Customer’s date of birth.
    • Whether alcohol will be served.
    • If the event requires a special permit.
    • The rate for the detail.

    These questions are custom fields and may vary depending on the agency. If no such fields appear, this section will not appear in your detail request form. After filling out the necessary fields, click Detail Information to continue.

    Step 5: Enter Detail Information


    Specify the duties the officers are expected to perform for this detail. Enter the Number of Officers required for the event. Indicate whether a patrol car is needed to perform the detail. You may add any notes for the officers in the Notes field. Click Detail Location to proceed.

    Step 6: Detail Location


    Enter the Address Line 1, Address Line 2 (optional), City, State, and Zip Code for the detail location. You can also add an optional location description if necessary. Once done, click Schedule to proceed to the next step.

    Step 7: Schedule the Detail


    Use the calendar tool to select the Start Date and End Date of the detail. Input the start and end times for the detail if required. Click Review to continue.

    Step 8: Review the Detail

    Review all of the entered information for accuracy, including contact details, additional information, and schedule. If any changes are necessary, you can use the Back button to go to previous sections and make adjustments. Once all details are correct, click Submit to submit your detail request.

    Step 9: Confirmation


    After submitting, you will see a confirmation message indicating that the request was submitted successfully. You can choose to view the Scheduled Detail or return to the Dashboard from the confirmation page.

    If you encounter any difficulties during the process, please contact EDS Support at support@extradutysolutions.com or call us at (123) 456-7890. We're here to help ensure your detail requests are processed smoothly and efficiently.

    Payments Section

    The Payments section allows you to view and manage payments made on your account. From this page, you can track payment statuses, view invoice details, and access historical payment data. The table below presents an organized view of all payment-related information.

    Features of the Payments Section:

    • Status: Displays the payment status for each invoice.
    • Invoice Number: The unique identifier for each invoice.
    • Customer Number: The associated customer number for the account.
    • Customer Name: Displays the name of the customer or business.
    • Invoice Date: The date the invoice was issued.
    • Due Date: The date by which the payment must be made.
    • Days Past Due: Displays the number of days an invoice has been overdue.
    • Invoice Total: The total amount billed on the invoice.
    • Balance Due: The remaining balance that needs to be paid.
    • Last Payment Amount: The amount of the last payment made on the invoice.
    • Last Payment Date: The date when the last payment was made.
    • Agency Name: The name of the agency associated with the invoice.

    Functions Above the Toolbar:

    • Refresh List: Updates the table to reflect the most recent payment activity.
    • View Payment: Displays detailed payment information for selected invoices.
    • Search: Search for specific invoices or payments using keywords such as invoice number, customer name, or agency name.
    • Filters: Apply filters to narrow down payment information based on criteria such as payment status or date range.
    • Groups: Organize and group the payment data by fields such as customer or agency name.
    • Table Settings: Customize the table layout by adding or removing columns to display the desired information.
    • Save Settings: Save your customized table settings for future use.

    If you have any issues or need assistance with payments, please contact EDS Support at support@extradutysolutions.com or call us at (123) 456-7890.
